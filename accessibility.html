Accessibility

Our commitment to an accessible website, built to WCAG 2.1 AA, with an on-page accessibility panel.

The Lichter Law Group is committed to making this website usable by everyone, including people who rely on assistive technology. We build to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

On this site

Use the accessibility button at the lower-left of any page to adjust text size, switch to a readable font, turn on high contrast, highlight links, enlarge the cursor, stop animations, or hide images. Your settings are saved on your device. The site also supports full keyboard navigation, visible focus indicators, descriptive alt text, and reduced-motion preferences.

Tell us

If you encounter any barrier, email info@ejlpa.com or call 305.894.6750 and we'll work to fix it and provide the information you need in an accessible way.
